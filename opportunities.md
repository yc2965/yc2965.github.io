# Opportunities

I am actively recruiting Ph.D. students and postdoctoral researchers interested in:

- Boundary-layer meteorology and turbulence  
- Land–atmosphere interactions and climate extremes  
- Machine learning and generative AI for Earth system modeling  
- Climate adaptation and geoengineering  

## Ph.D. Students

Prospective students should have a strong background in atmospheric science, fluid mechanics, physics, applied mathematics, or a related field. Experience with numerical modeling (LES, DNS, climate models) and/or machine learning is highly encouraged.

## Postdoctoral Researchers

I welcome applicants with expertise in turbulence theory, land–climate interactions, high-resolution modeling, or AI-driven Earth system modeling.

## How to Apply

Please email me at **yu_cheng@fudan.edu.cn** with:

- CV  
- Brief statement of research interests  
- Contact information for 2–3 references
